About the Institute of Culinary Education

Award-winning, vibrant and diverse, the Institute of Culinary Education has helped thousands of students find their culinary voices — and we're ready to help you find yours. With campuses in dynamic and exciting food cities — New York City and Los Angeles — ICE works at the forefront of the food and hospitality industries.
